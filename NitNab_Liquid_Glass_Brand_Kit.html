Direction 05 — Material: Liquid Glass

Liquid Glass

macOS Tahoe's defining design material applied to NitNab. Translucent surfaces, backdrop blur, refracting tints. The tint color shifts contextually: .blue for transcripts, .indigo for AI, .purple for chat, .green for privacy, .orange for audio.

Design Rationale

Liquid Glass is Tahoe's signature. NitNab targets macOS 26+ exclusively — it can embrace this material fully. The translucency communicates transparency: you can see through the glass, just as you can see through NitNab's privacy commitments. Nothing is hidden. The contextual tint system maps each of NitNab's three tabs to a system color: Transcript (blue, for content), Summary (indigo, for AI), Chat (purple, for conversation). This creates a gentle ambient color shift as users navigate the app. Liquid Glass materials are implemented via SwiftUI's .glassEffect() modifier, ensuring the app renders identically to first-party Tahoe apps.
